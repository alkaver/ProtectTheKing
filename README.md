# ProtectTheKing 🏰👑
## ProtectTheKing is a strategic survival game built in Unity, where your main objective is to defend the king at all costs. If either you or the king dies, the game ends.
## 🌐 Play Now
### You can play the game directly in your browser: [ProtectTheKing](https://alkaver.itch.io/protect-the-king)
## 🕹️ How to Play
### Survive 20 turns while defending the king.
### Every 5 turns, a boss appears. Defeating it lets you choose a new ability.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1b3118e9-3135-4e2a-bce1-ecbf443474ce)
### After regular turns, visit the shop to upgrade your stats and the king’s.
![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8adde0c5-298c-4ecc-b006-158b87f1a6b6)
Strategically manage your skills and resources to ensure victory!


## 🛠️ Built With
### Unity – for game development
### WebGL – for browser compatibility
